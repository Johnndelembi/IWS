Our Objectives

We hope to contribute to improving health outcomes, reducing health costs, increasing productivity within the community and workforce, and to foster positive social change. This will complement the government's current efforts towards the Health Sector Strategic Plan, July 2021 - June 2026. This is an effort to create a healthy and prosperous society that contributes fully to the development of both the individual and the nation as well as to control communicable diseases and improve reproductive health and the health of mothers and children, similar to the Sustainable Development Goal 3 (SDG 3) which aims to ensure healthy lives and promote the well-being of individuals of all ages.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive wellness Consultancies in fostering a workplace culture of wellness to increase the productivity and welfare of the employees, specifically in the following key areas.

Managerial Consultation..

Managerial Consultation psycho socio issues ranging from stress management, crisis management, assistance in managing risk cases, cultural diversity, or performance management of employees, and review of policies to ensure a conducive legal framework to promote the well-being of employees and the company.

Emergency Response..

Emergency Response Critical Incident Stress Debriefing (CISD) Worksite Counseling and Stress Management Support in times of emergency such as the death of a colleague or disaster at a worksite.

Professional Telephone Counselling..

Professional Telephone Counselling in two languages, both Swahili and English - Counselling for marital and parenting-related psychosocial problems, substance abuse and addiction, relationship problems, financial management, health, personal, or work-related problems, and all mental disorders.

Physical wellness

Support physical wellness that encourages the balance of physical activity, nutrition, good sleep, and mental well-being.

Professional Face-to-Face Counselling

Professional Face-to-Face Counselling - For any psychological, relationship, medical, stress, adolescent counseling, family therapy, individual and corporate coaching financial problem, marital & pre-natal counseling, trauma-focused counseling, , fitness, nutrition, and lifestyle management.

Education Support and Awareness..

Education Support and Awareness - Conduct awareness programs across the network to equip employees on key psychological threats/sign periodically.

Arrangements for Home-Based Care Services..

Arrangements for Home-Based Care Services - Support of home health services where needed.

Wellness Days...

Support in conducting annual wellness days across the network that help to keep employees well educated in various health issues etc.
